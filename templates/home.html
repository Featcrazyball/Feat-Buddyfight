Introducing the Website

In this website, there are various functions to allow the user to enjoy Future Card Buddyfight such that one can play the TCG without stepping out of their house.

What is Future Card Buddyfight? Future Card Buddyfight is a Trading Card Game created by Bushiroad.

Open the flag!

Should one have the desire to visit their childhood game known as Buddyfight, this website is the best option for your needs.

Gacha Time

In order to create a similar experience for opening packs in real life, we have implemented a gacha system to unlock cards.

When you create a deck and attempt to edit it, you may realise that it only shows cards that are not only applicable, but also ones that you have unlocked via the gacha system.

You need tickets to roll, tickets are earned via gameplay. You unlock all trial deck cards when your account is first created.

Make your own Deck!

Similar to Buddyfight, where you make your own decks, it is possible on the website.

There are various decks and worlds that one can use. Including Dragon Ein, Searing Executioners, and many more!

Hurry and come over, make your deck, and join the fun

Deck Building

With cards that you have unlocked, build your own deck to fight other players!

Lobby

It does not matter if you have a friend to play with or not, just make some friends.

The lobby allows you to find other lonely adventurers who want to play Buddyfight as well.

Should you be bored and like to spectate other's matches, click the "Available Matches" to be directed to the list of games currently occuring.

However, please remember to build your deck first
